HOW THE PROJECT WORKS...

NEURO GRIP is a cutting-edge assistive technology developed to help individuals with paralysis regain functional hand mobility. By harnessing the power of Arduino microcontrollers, logic-level controllers (LLCs), and a network of intelligent sensors, NEURO GRIP detects subtle neural or muscular signals — even in weakened or partially responsive limbs — and translates them into real-time physical hand movements. This system bridges the gap between the brain’s intent and the body’s response, enabling users to perform daily tasks that once seemed impossible. More than just a piece of hardware, NEURO GRIP represents the intersection of engineering, neuroscience, and empathy — a solution designed not only to assist movement but to restore independence, dignity, and quality of life for those affected by motor impairments.
