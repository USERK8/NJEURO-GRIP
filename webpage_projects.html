WELCOME TO NEURO GRIP

Neuro Grip is an innovative assistive technology project built to help individuals with paralysis regain hand mobility. It combines Arduino, logic-level controllers (LLCs), and smart sensors to detect subtle neural or muscular signals and translate them into real-time hand movements.

HOW THE PROJECT WORKS...

NEURO GRIP detects weak neural or muscular signals — even in partially responsive limbs — through sensors. These signals are processed by Arduino microcontrollers and LLCs, which then activate actuators to move the hand.

This bridges the gap between the brain’s intent and the body’s response, allowing users to perform daily tasks once thought impossible.

More than just hardware, NEURO GRIP represents the intersection of engineering, neuroscience, and empathy — restoring independence, dignity, and quality of life.

WHAT ISSUE DOES THE PROJECT SOLVE...?

Millions of people suffer from paralysis due to accidents or neurological conditions. Simple tasks like holding a pen, eating, or gripping objects become extremely challenging.

NEURO GRIP directly addresses this issue by creating a low-cost, portable, and effective solution that restores essential hand functions — empowering individuals to live with more independence and confidence.
